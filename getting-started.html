Questions, answered

How should I choose a listing price?

Select whatever listing price works for you! Our tool is just a suggestion based on historical trends and data.

What are some sites that I can list on?

There are a couple sites we suggest to rent out your property. Airbnb, Clickstay, Wimdu, Booking.com. Though, our pricing suggestions are tailored to the Airbnb platform.

Does your suggested price include taxes and fees?

No! Taxes and fees such as cleaning, for late check-in, pets, etc. are at your discretion and dependent your overseeing government.

What areas does your tool work with?

Currently, our tool is tailored to properties located in Toyko and the greater Tokyo area. Let us know what recommendations you have on where we should focus next!
